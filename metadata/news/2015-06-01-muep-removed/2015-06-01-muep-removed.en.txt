News-Item-Format: 1.0

::muep[1] has now been removed from ::unavailable-unofficial, due to being
unmaintained for a long time now.

If you use any packages from ::muep, you can resurrect them at any time
by following the instructions on our Contributing[2] doc, and maintain them
in your own repository.

[1]: http://gitorious.org/muep-exherbo/muep-exherbo
[2]: https://exherbo.org/docs/contributing.html
