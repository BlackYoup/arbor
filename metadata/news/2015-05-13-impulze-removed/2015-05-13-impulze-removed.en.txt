Title: repository/impulze has been removed from ::unavailable-unofficial
Author: Kylie McClain <somasissounds@gmail.com>
Content-Type: text/plain
Posted: 2015-05-13
Revision: 1
News-Item-Format: 1.0
Display-If-Installed: repository/impulze

::impulze[1] has now been removed from ::unavailable-unofficial, due to being
unmaintained for a while now.

If you use any packages from ::impulze, you can resurrect them at any time
by following the instructions on our Contributing[2] doc, and maintain them
in your own repository.

[1]: https://github.com/impulze/exherbo-impulze
[2]: https://exherbo.org/docs/contributing.html
